Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Chronic Kidney Disease and our AI system

What is Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)?

CKD is a long-term condition where the kidneys gradually lose function. It can progress to kidney failure without early detection and treatment.

How does this AI tool predict CKD?

Our tool uses machine learning algorithms trained on real medical data to predict CKD risk based on user inputs like lab reports and symptoms.

Is the prediction 100% accurate?

While highly reliable, this tool is meant for screening, not diagnosis. Always consult a medical professional before taking action.

What are common symptoms of CKD?

Early CKD may have no symptoms. As it progresses, symptoms may include fatigue, swelling, changes in urination, and high blood pressure.

Can CKD be reversed?

CKD cannot usually be reversed, but early detection and proper management can slow progression and improve quality of life.

How do I get a personalized diet plan?

Once you input your lab values and choose your dietary preferences, the AI generates a kidney-safe diet plan tailored to your condition.
